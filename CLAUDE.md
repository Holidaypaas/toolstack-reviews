# CLAUDE.md

- All editorial content must follow [WRITING-STYLE.md](WRITING-STYLE.md). Read it in full before writing or editing any article in `src/content/` or any page copy in `src/pages/`. The em-dash budget (max 2 per article) and banned-phrase list are hard rules.
- Voice reference: `src/content/reviews/systeme-io-review.mdx`.
- Placeholder claims in seed content carry `{/* TODO */}` comments. Never remove a TODO without replacing the claim with verified data.
- Affiliate links never point at vendors directly; always `/go/<slug>/` (see `src/config/affiliates.ts`).
