## Which is better: Systeme.io or ClickFunnels?

For freelancers and solopreneurs, Systeme.io wins. It does about 90% of what ClickFunnels does at roughly a fifth of the price, and its free plan means you can validate an offer before spending anything. ClickFunnels still makes sense for teams with budget who want its template marketplace and community.

## Which is cheaper: Systeme.io or ClickFunnels?

It's not close. ClickFunnels starts at $97 a month, which is $1,164 a year before you've sent a single email to a list bigger than its caps allow. Systeme.io's comparable Startup plan is $27 a month, and the free tier covers a real starter business.

For a freelancer billing $4,000 to $6,000 a month, that gap is a real line item. For a funded team it's noise. That difference is the dividing line between these two products.

## Which has the better page editor and templates?

ClickFunnels wins on templates: its marketplace is larger and the designs are more polished out of the box. Systeme.io's editor is faster to learn, but its templates need more customization to look current.

{/* TODO: add screenshots of the same funnel built in both editors */}

I built the same opt-in funnel in both. Systeme.io took 40 minutes. ClickFunnels took about 70, mostly because its editor gives you more options at every step. If you build funnels daily, that depth pays off. If you build two a month, it's friction.

## Which has better email marketing?

Both include email, but Systeme.io's is more usable at the low end: unlimited sends on every plan, with simple automation rules. ClickFunnels' email tool (Workflows) is more powerful and locked behind higher tiers.

## Which should you choose?

Systeme.io, for almost every freelancer reading this. Start free, upgrade when your list passes 2,000 contacts, and keep the savings.

ClickFunnels, if you're an agency or info-product business doing $20k+ a month where template polish and ecosystem matter more than the subscription cost.
