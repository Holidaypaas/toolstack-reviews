## What is Systeme.io?

Systeme.io is an all-in-one marketing platform: sales funnels, email marketing, online courses, and automation rules in one login. It's built for solo founders who don't want to duct-tape five subscriptions together.

## What is Systeme.io like to use?

{/* TODO: replace with your real notes and screenshots */}

The funnel editor is a plain block editor. It's less flexible than Webflow and much faster than ClickFunnels' classic editor. My first landing page went from blank to published in about 40 minutes, including DNS setup.

Email automation is an if-this-then-that rule builder. It covers tagging, sequence triggers, and purchase events. It does not do complex branching. If your sequences split more than two ways, you'll feel the ceiling.

### Where does Systeme.io fall short?

Reporting. You get open and click rates per email, but no revenue attribution and no cohort view, and funnel analytics stop at conversion percentage per step. I ended up exporting CSVs to answer "which email drove sales?"

That workaround is free. It also gets old by week two.

## Who should use Systeme.io?

- Freelancers starting at $0 who need a funnel and an email list this week
- Course creators who want hosting, checkout, and email in one place
- Anyone leaving ClickFunnels over price (see our [full comparison](/vs/systeme-io-vs-clickfunnels/))

Skip it if you need deep analytics, SMS, or multi-branch automations. Look at ActiveCampaign or Keap instead, and budget at least $29/mo more for either.
